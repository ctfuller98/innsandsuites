Wedding Receptions

Here at The Temple Inn and Suites, we are dedicated to ensuring that your wedding reception is unforgettable. We have a variety of services to make your planning stress-free.

Services Offered
All wedding packages include the cost of setup and cleanup services, linens, tables, chairs, flatware, glassware, china, and any other related food and beverage items. If desired, you can rent an LCD projector and screen, a DVD player, a lectern or podium, a wireless microphone system, and an LCD television. All hotel areas are wheelchair accessible.

Cuisine
All catering menus can be customized to meet the needs of your budget, preferences, and dietary needs. Buffet and plated options are available. Hors d'oeuvres can be served passed or in stations.Cake cutting service is always complimentary.
